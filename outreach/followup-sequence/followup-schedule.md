## Placeholders to Swap Before Sending

| Placeholder | Replace With |
|-------------|-------------|
| `[FIRST_NAME]` | Prospect's first name |
| `[COMPANY]` | Company name |
| `[INDUSTRY]` | Industry vertical (e.g. "property management", "accounting", "construction") |

---

## Exit Conditions

- **Prospect replies** → Remove from sequence immediately, handle manually
- **Prospect books a call** → Remove from sequence, move to discovery pipeline
- **Day 14 sent with no reply** → Move to 30-day nurture list

---

## Sequence Performance Targets

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Open rate (each email) | > 40% |
| Reply rate (across sequence) | > 8% |
| Booking rate (across sequence) | > 3% |
| Best-performing email | Track and double down |
