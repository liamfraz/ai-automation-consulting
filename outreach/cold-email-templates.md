## Template Selection Guide

| Scenario | Template | Why |
|----------|----------|-----|
| You can see a specific manual process on their site/reviews | **A** (Pain-Point) | Specificity drives response rates |
| You have a relevant case study for their vertical | **B** (Social Proof) | Proof reduces perceived risk |
| Cold lead, limited intel on their operations | **C** (Free Value) | Low barrier, audit does the discovery |
| High-value prospect, worth extra research | **A** then **B** | Personalise with pain point, back up with proof |

## Sending Cadence

- **Day 1 (Monday/Tuesday AM):** Send initial email. Best open rates are Tue–Thu, 8–10am local time.
- **Day 3 (Wednesday/Thursday AM):** Follow-up. Reference original email. Add a specific time suggestion.
- **Day 7 (following Monday/Tuesday AM):** Final touch. Light, no-pressure. Leave the door open.
- **After Day 7:** Move to nurture list. Don't email again for 30 days minimum.

## Metrics to Track

- Open rate (aim: >40% — subject lines are working)
- Reply rate (aim: >5% — message resonates)
- Booking rate (aim: >2% — CTA is clear)
- Which template variant drives the most bookings (not just opens)
