## Search Tips

- **Filter by:** Posted in last 24 hours, Payment verified, Budget > $500
- **Skip:** Jobs with 20+ proposals already (unless perfect fit)
- **Prioritize:** Jobs posted < 4 hours ago with < 5 proposals
- **Save searches:** Upwork lets you save searches and get email alerts — set up alerts for Tier 1 queries
- **Time your searches:** Check at 6-8am AEST (catches US evening posts) and 8-10pm AEST (catches US morning posts)
- **Combine with filters:** Add "intermediate" or "expert" experience level to avoid low-budget jobs
