# Upwork Proposal Templates

## How to Use These Templates

1. **Read the job post carefully** — identify the specific pain point and any named tools/platforms
2. **Pick the closest template** below and customize the bracketed sections
3. **Always reference their specific situation** in the opening line — generic proposals get ignored
4. **Keep it under 300 words** — hiring managers skim

## Proposal Checklist

Before sending any proposal, verify:

- [ ] Opening line references something specific from THEIR job post (not generic)
- [ ] You've mentioned a relevant portfolio project (FlowGen AI, AU Business Chatbot, or Review Responder)
- [ ] Timeline is realistic and specific
- [ ] Pricing is included (fixed or hourly)
- [ ] Call to action is clear (suggest a call or ask a specific question)
- [ ] Under 300 words (trim ruthlessly)
- [ ] No typos or placeholder brackets left unfilled
- [ ] You've answered their key questions before they ask them
