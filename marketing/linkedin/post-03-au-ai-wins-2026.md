The 3 biggest AI automation wins I've seen for AU small businesses in 2026.

---

**1. Invoice processing — Melbourne trades company**

The owner was personally reviewing and approving every invoice before payment. 80+ per month. Saturday mornings, mostly.

We built an AI layer that reads incoming invoices, matches them to purchase orders, flags discrepancies, and routes anything under $500 for auto-approval.

Result: 14 hours/month back. Zero missed early-payment discounts. Owner hasn't touched an invoice since March.

The ROI hit in week 2.

---

**2. Customer onboarding — Brisbane professional services firm**

New clients were sent a 6-email sequence manually by the admin team. Each email was copy-pasted and personalised by hand. Every. Single. Time.

Now: client signs the contract → AI triggers the full onboarding sequence, personalised to their service tier, pulls their details from the CRM, schedules the kickoff call, and sends the welcome pack.

Zero manual steps. Admin team reallocated to actual client work.

3 hours saved per new client. They onboard ~12/month.

---

**3. HR compliance workflows — Sydney services business (20 staff)**

Leave requests, timesheet approvals, and compliance sign-offs were managed over email. Things got missed. Staff were frustrated. HR was drowning.

Power Automate + a simple AI classifier now handles the entire flow: request comes in → categorised → routed to the right approver → reminder sent if no response in 48hrs → logged automatically.

HR went from reactive to invisible. The system just runs.

---

The pattern across all three:

→ The problem wasn't complexity — it was repetition
→ The fix was always simpler than the business expected
→ The ROI showed up in the first month, not the first quarter
→ None of them replaced existing tools or bought new software
